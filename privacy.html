Policy.

FocuzNow is built around a local-first approach. This page explains the information involved when you use our website, extension, and support channels.

Effective July 20, 2026Last updated July 20, 2026

1. Information you provide

We receive information you choose to provide, such as an email address when you create an account or contact us. If you use support, we also receive the information included in your message.

2. Focus data

FocuzNow is designed so that focus activity, preferences, plans, and blocking configuration can remain in your local workspace. Features that require an account or an online request may process the information needed to provide that feature.

3. How information is used

We use information to operate the product, respond to requests, maintain security, improve reliability, and communicate important service updates. We do not use your focus data to sell targeted advertising.

4. Sharing

We share information only when needed to provide the service, comply with law, protect rights and safety, or complete a business transaction involving FocuzNow. Service providers may process information on our behalf under appropriate safeguards.

5. Your choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of account information by contacting us. Local data can generally be managed from the browser or extension controls available to you.

6. Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to hello@focuznow.com. You can also review our Terms of Service.

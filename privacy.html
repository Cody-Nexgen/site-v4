Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED — MAY 31, 2026

Your attention is yours, and so is your data. This Privacy Policy explains what Focuznow collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Focuznow, you agree to the practices described here.

The short version: Most of Focuznow runs entirely on your device. Your browsing statistics, blocklists, habits, and notes stay local by default. We don't sell your data — ever.

1. What We Collect

  • On-device data. Your blocklists, focus statistics, habits, scratches, calendar events, and settings are stored locally in your browser. We cannot see this unless you explicitly enable sync.
  • Account data (Pro). If you create an account, we store your email and subscription status.
  • AI Coach interactions. When you use the AI Coach, the messages you send are processed to generate responses and perform the actions you request.
  • Basic diagnostics. Anonymous, aggregated crash and performance data to keep the extension healthy.

2. What We Don't Collect

We do not track your full browsing history, sell your data to third parties, or build advertising profiles. Site-time statistics used for the Statistics tool are computed and stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide and improve the Service.
  • To process AI Coach requests and apply the changes you ask for.
  • To manage Pro subscriptions and respond to support requests.
  • To keep the Service secure and diagnose problems.

4. Optional Sync

If you enable sync, your settings and data are encrypted and stored so they can move between your devices. You can disable sync and delete synced data at any time.

5. Third Parties

We use a small number of trusted providers for payment processing, AI inference, and infrastructure. They process data only as needed to perform their services and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

6. Your Rights

You can access, export, or delete your data at any time from the extension's settings. If you have an account, you may request deletion by contacting us. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA.

7. Data Retention

On-device data persists until you remove it or uninstall the extension. Account data is retained while your account is active and deleted on request.

8. Children

Focuznow is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be announced within the Service. Continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Privacy questions? Email privacy@focuznow.app.
